What is Cash Flux?

Cash Flux is a financial planning and management web application. Cash Flux can store data about your income/outcome, in an easy to read table, much like a bank statement. With that data you can visualize spending habits and trends through an assortment of graphs allowing you to more easily plan budgets and save more efficiently.
